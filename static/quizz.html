Quantum Physics Quiz

Question 1: What is quantum superposition?

Answer: Quantum superposition is the concept that a particle can exist in multiple states or locations simultaneously.

Question 2: What is entanglement?

Answer: Entanglement is a phenomenon where two or more particles become linked in such a way that their states are dependent on each other, no matter how far apart they are in space.

Question 3: What is the behavior of particles in the quantum realm?

Answer: The behavior of particles in the quantum realm is probabilistic, meaning that it is impossible to predict with certainty the outcome of a measurement or observation of a particle's state.
