Quantum Physics Quiz

Question 1

What is the concept of quantum superposition?

Quantum superposition is the concept that a particle can exist in multiple states or locations simultaneously.

Question 2

What is entanglement?

Entanglement is where two or more particles can become linked in such a way that their states are dependent on each other, no matter how far apart they are in space.

Question 3

What is the behavior of particles in the quantum realm?

The behavior of particles in the quantum realm is probabilistic, meaning that it is impossible to predict with certainty the outcome of a measurement or observation of a particle's state.
