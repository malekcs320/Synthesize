Quiz:

Question 1:

What is the concept of quantum superposition?

A: Quantum superposition is the idea that a particle can exist in multiple states or locations simultaneously.

Question 2:

What is entanglement?

A: Entanglement is when two or more particles become linked in such a way that their states are dependent on each other, no matter how far apart they are in space.

Question 3:

What equation is used to calculate the probability of various outcomes in quantum physics?

A: The Schrödinger equation is used to calculate the probability of various outcomes in quantum physics.
